Gravy Recipe

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • a bit of fresh or dried parsley (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Melt the butter in a small sauce pan. Add flour, whisking until smooth for about 3-4 minutes. Slowly whisk in the beef broth and worcestershire sauce, and bring to a simmer for 5-10 minutes or to your desired thickness. Add cream, parsley (if chosen) and voila! Salt and pepper accordingly.
